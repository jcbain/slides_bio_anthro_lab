Anthropometry

 

the measurement of the human body

 

MAJOR COMPONENT TO STUDIES OF HUMAN VARIATION

somatometry--> measurement of living humans osteometry--> measurement of skeletal remains

MEASUREMENTS

 

ANTHROPOMETRY USES QUANTITATIVE MEASURES

think numbers

not subjective

 
 

inter and intra-observer error

inter--> measurement inconsistencies between observers

intra--> measurement inconsistencies between a single observer

 

 

Precision

 

the closeness of repeated measurements to each other

 

Accuracy

 

the closeness of the repeated measurements to the true value
